## Welcome to the Heart of Trading

Trading isn't just about candlesticks, indicators, or risk-reward ratios.
Behind every trade, every decision, every win and loss — <Highlight variant="yellow">there's a human being</Highlight> with dreams, fears, setbacks, and triumphs.

In this module, you'll step away from the technical analysis and dive into something far more powerful: <Highlight variant="red">the stories that shaped the traders themselves</Highlight>.<br/>
I've sat down with traders from all walks of life — some who've been navigating the markets for decades, weathering multiple financial crises
and reinventing themselves time and again, and others who discovered trading just two years ago and are already rewriting their life stories.
What they all share is something invaluable: <Highlight variant="yellow">lived experience</Highlight>.

These aren't your typical "how I made my first million" interviews. These are raw, honest conversations about:

**<CheckCircle className="w-4 h-4 inline" stroke="#22c55e" />** The personal battles that tested their resolve<br/>
**<CheckCircle className="w-4 h-4 inline" stroke="#22c55e" />** The moments of doubt that nearly made them quit<br/>
**<CheckCircle className="w-4 h-4 inline" stroke="#22c55e" />** The life experiences that shaped their trading psychology<br/>
**<CheckCircle className="w-4 h-4 inline" stroke="#22c55e" />** The values and beliefs that guide their decision-making<br/>
**<CheckCircle className="w-4 h-4 inline" stroke="#22c55e" />** The real person behind the trading account<br/>

You'll hear about childhood experiences that influenced risk tolerance, career failures that led to trading success, relationships
that were tested by the markets, and the defining moments that transformed how they see both trading and life.
