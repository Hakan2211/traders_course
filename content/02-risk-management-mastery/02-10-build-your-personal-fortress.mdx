## THE FOUR TRADING STYLES: UNDERSTANDING YOUR WAR

Before we customize, let's deeply understand the <Highlight variant="yellow">four primary trading styles</Highlight> and their risk implications.

## STYLE 1: THE SCALPER (Rapid Strike Specialist)

### <Highlight variant="accent">THE SCALPER'S WAR</Highlight>

**<Highlight variant="yellow">Analogy:</Highlight>** You're a special forces operative conducting rapid raids behind enemy lines.
Get in, secure the objective, get out. Minutes, not hours. Hundreds of operations per month.

**<Highlight variant="blue">Trading profile:</Highlight>**

→ **Duration:** Seconds to minutes (rarely >15 minutes)<br/>
→ **Trades per day:** 10-100+ (high frequency)<br/>
→ **Target per trade:** 5-15 pips (forex) / 0.1-0.3% (stocks)<br/>
→ **Stop loss:** Very tight (5-15 pips / 0.1-0.2%)<br/>
→ **Risk-reward typical:** 1:1 to 1:1.5<br/>
→ **Win rate needed:** High (60-70%+)<br/>
→ **Session:** Intense focus for 2-4 hour blocks<br/>

### <Highlight variant="accent">SCALPER'S RISK FRAMEWORK</Highlight>

**<Highlight variant="yellow">POSITION SIZING:</Highlight>**

**<Highlight variant="yellow">Base risk per trade:</Highlight>** 0.1-0.5% (very small due to high frequency)

**<Highlight variant="blue">Example:</Highlight>**

→ $10,000 account<br/>
→ Risk per trade: 0.2% = $20<br/>
→ Stop: 10 pips<br/>
→ Position size: 0.02 lots (2,000 units EUR/USD)<br/>

Even at 100 trades/day, max theoretical risk = 20% (won't happen with 60%+ win rate)<br/>

**<Highlight variant="blue">Why so small?</Highlight>**

→ Death by a thousand cuts is real<br/>
→ One bad day with 0.5% risk × 50 trades = 25% potential loss<br/>
→ Spreads/commissions eat larger % of small targets<br/>
→ Need buffer for inevitable losing streaks<br/>

**<Highlight variant="red">STOP LOSS PLACEMENT:</Highlight>**

**<Highlight variant="blue">Method:</Highlight>** Technical (support/resistance) + ATR

**<Highlight variant="blue">Typical:</Highlight>** 1.5-2× ATR (very tight)

**<Highlight variant="blue">Example:</Highlight>**

→ EUR/USD ATR: 60 pips (daily)<br/>
→ Scalp ATR (5-min chart): 8 pips<br/>
→ Stop distance: 1.5 × 8 = 12 pips<br/>

**<Highlight variant="red">Critical:</Highlight>** Stop MUST be beyond spread<br/>
→ If spread is 1 pip, don't use 5-pip stops (20% eaten immediately)<br/>

**<Highlight variant="blue">Why strict daily limits?</Highlight>**

→ Scalpers can destroy accounts in a single session<br/>
→ 50 losing trades at 0.5% = -25% in one day (catastrophic)<br/>
→ Mental fatigue after 3-4 hours leads to errors<br/>
→ Circuit breakers are LIFE OR DEATH for scalpers<br/>

**<Highlight variant="yellow">PORTFOLIO HEAT:</Highlight>**

**<Highlight variant="blue">Typical:</Highlight>** 1-3 positions maximum simultaneously

**<Highlight variant="blue">Why so few?</Highlight>**

→ Need laser focus on each position<br/>
→ Can't monitor 8 scalp positions at once<br/>
→ Spreads multiply with more positions<br/>
→ One setup, execute perfectly, move to next<br/>

**<Highlight variant="yellow">Portfolio heat:</Highlight>** 0.5-1.5% maximum

### <Highlight variant="accent">SCALPER'S COMPLETE FRAMEWORK</Highlight>

**<Highlight variant="yellow">SCALPER'S RISK MANAGEMENT PLAN</Highlight>**

**<Highlight variant="blue">TRADING HOURS:</Highlight>**

→ London Open (3am-7am EST) OR NY Open (9:30am-11:30am EST)<br/>
→ Maximum 4-hour sessions<br/>

**<Highlight variant="blue">POSITION SIZING:</Highlight>**

→ Risk per trade: 0.2% ($20 on $10k account)<br/>
→ Max simultaneous: 2 positions<br/>
→ Portfolio heat limit: 0.4%<br/>

**<Highlight variant="red">STOP LOSS:</Highlight>**

→ Method: 1.5× 5-minute ATR<br/>
→ Typical: 8-12 pips<br/>
→ NEVER wider than 15 pips<br/>
→ Always account for spread (1-2 pips)<br/>

**<Highlight variant="blue">TARGETS:</Highlight>**

→ Primary: 1:1 to 1.5× ATR<br/>
→ Typical: 10-15 pips<br/>
→ Scale out: 50% at 1:1, 50% at 1:1.5<br/>

**<Highlight variant="red">CIRCUIT BREAKERS:</Highlight>**

**<AlertTriangle className="w-4 h-4 inline" stroke="#f59e0b" />** 5 consecutive losses → 15-minute break<br/>
**<Circle className="w-4 h-4 inline" stroke="red" fill="red" />** -1.5% daily loss → STOP FOR DAY<br/>
**<Circle className="w-4 h-4 inline" stroke="red" fill="red" />** -4% weekly loss → STOP FOR WEEK<br/>
**<Circle className="w-4 h-4 inline" stroke="red" fill="red" />** Max 50 trades per day<br/>

**<Highlight variant="blue">PERFORMANCE TARGETS:</Highlight>**

→ Daily: +1-2R<br/>
→ Weekly: +5-8R<br/>
→ Monthly: +15-25R<br/>
→ Win rate: 60%+ required<br/>

**<Highlight variant="blue">TOOLS:</Highlight>**

→ Platform: High-speed execution (cTrader, MT5, proprietary)<br/>
→ Charts: 1-min, 5-min<br/>
→ Indicators: Price action, order flow, Level II<br/>
→ Execution: Hotkeys essential<br/>

**<Highlight variant="blue">REVIEW SCHEDULE:</Highlight>**

→ Daily: End-of-session review (30 min)<br/>
→ Weekly: Full performance audit (1 hour)<br/>
→ Monthly: Strategy refinement<br/>

**<Highlight variant="red">FORBIDDEN:</Highlight>**

**<X className="w-4 h-4 inline" stroke="red" />** Trading during low liquidity (lunch hour, late sessions)<br/>
**<X className="w-4 h-4 inline" stroke="red" />** Holding positions >30 minutes<br/>
**<X className="w-4 h-4 inline" stroke="red" />** Trading during high-impact news<br/>
**<X className="w-4 h-4 inline" stroke="red" />** Revenge trading after 3 losses<br/>
**<X className="w-4 h-4 inline" stroke="red" />** Increasing size after wins<br/>

## STYLE 2: THE DAY TRADER (Tactical Campaigner)

### <Highlight variant="accent">THE DAY TRADER'S WAR</Highlight>

**<Highlight variant="yellow">Analogy:</Highlight>** You're a tactical commander leading daily campaigns. Plan in the morning, execute during the day, return to base by evening. No overnight occupation.

**<Highlight variant="blue">Trading profile:</Highlight>**

→ **Duration:** Minutes to hours (always closed by EOD)<br/>
→ **Trades per day:** 1-10<br/>
→ **Target per trade:** 20-80 pips / 0.5-2%<br/>
→ **Stop loss:** Moderate (20-50 pips / 0.3-1%)<br/>
→ **Risk-reward typical:** 1:1.5 to 1:2.5<br/>
→ **Win rate target:** 45-55%<br/>
→ **Session:** 6-8 hours (morning analysis + trading + review)<br/>

### <Highlight variant="accent">DAY TRADER'S RISK FRAMEWORK</Highlight>

**<Highlight variant="yellow">POSITION SIZING:</Highlight>**

**<Highlight variant="yellow">Base risk per trade:</Highlight>** 0.5-1% (moderate)<br/>

**<Highlight variant="blue">Example:</Highlight>**

→ $10,000 account<br/>
→ Risk per trade: 1% = $100<br/>
→ Stop: 40 pips<br/>
→ Position size: 0.025 lots (2,500 units EUR/USD)<br/>

**<Highlight variant="blue">With 5 trades/day average:</Highlight>**

→ Max theoretical daily risk: 5%
→ With 50% win rate: Typical daily = ±1-2R

**<Highlight variant="red">STOP LOSS PLACEMENT:</Highlight>**

**<Highlight variant="blue">Method:</Highlight>** Hybrid (Technical + ATR)

**<Highlight variant="blue">Typical:</Highlight>** 2-3× 15-minute ATR

**<Highlight variant="blue">Example:</Highlight>**

→ EUR/USD 15-min ATR: 15 pips<br/>
→ Stop distance: 2.5 × 15 = 37.5 pips<br/>
→ Round to: 40 pips<br/>

**<Highlight variant="blue">Alternative:</Highlight>** Support/resistance with 10-pip buffer

→ Support at 1.0950<br/>
→ Stop at 1.0940<br/>

**<Highlight variant="yellow">DAILY LIMITS:</Highlight>**

→ Max trades: 10 (prevents overtrading)<br/>
→ Max simultaneous: 4 positions<br/>
→ Portfolio heat: 4% maximum<br/>
→ Daily loss limit: -2.5% → STOP FOR DAY<br/>
→ Time stop: Close all by 3:45pm EST (before close)<br/>

**<Highlight variant="yellow">WEEKLY LIMITS:</Highlight>**

→ Max weekly loss: -6% → STOP FOR WEEK<br/>
→ Target: +8-12R per week<br/>

**<Highlight variant="yellow">MONTHLY LIMITS:</Highlight>**

→ Max monthly loss: -12% → REDUCE SIZE 50%<br/>
→ Target: +25-40R per month<br/>

**<Highlight variant="yellow">PORTFOLIO HEAT:</Highlight>**

→ Typical: 3-5 positions simultaneously<br/>
→ Portfolio heat limit: 4%<br/>

**<Highlight variant="blue">Example:</Highlight>**

→ Trade 1: EUR/USD (1% risk)<br/>
→ Trade 2: GBP/JPY (1% risk)<br/>
→ Trade 3: Gold (0.8% risk)<br/>
→ Trade 4: AAPL (1% risk)<br/>

Total: 3.8% (within limit)<br/>

**<Highlight variant="blue">Check correlation:</Highlight>**

→ EUR/USD + GBP/USD = high correlation (avoid)<br/>
→ EUR/USD + Gold + AAPL + GBP/JPY = low correlation (good)<br/>

### <Highlight variant="accent">DAY TRADER'S COMPLETE FRAMEWORK</Highlight>

**<Highlight variant="yellow">DAY TRADER'S RISK MANAGEMENT PLAN</Highlight>**

**<Highlight variant="blue">TRADING HOURS:</Highlight>**

→ Pre-market: 8:00am-9:30am (analysis, levels)<br/>
→ Active: 9:30am-3:45pm EST<br/>
→ All positions CLOSED by 3:45pm (no overnight)<br/>

**<Highlight variant="blue">POSITION SIZING:</Highlight>**

**<Highlight variant="yellow">POSITION SIZING:</Highlight>**

**<Highlight variant="yellow">Base risk per trade:</Highlight>** 1-2% (standard professional sizing)<br/>

**<Highlight variant="blue">Example:</Highlight>**

→ $10,000 account<br/>
→ **Risk per trade:** 1.5% = $150<br/>
→ **Stop:** 100 pips (4H chart support)<br/>
→ **Position size:** 0.015 lots (1,500 units EUR/USD)<br/>

**<Highlight variant="blue">With 3 positions open:</Highlight>**

→ Portfolio heat: 4.5% (within 6% limit)<br/>

**<Highlight variant="red">STOP LOSS PLACEMENT:</Highlight>**

**<Highlight variant="blue">Method:</Highlight>** Hybrid (4H/Daily structure + ATR)<br/>

**<Highlight variant="blue">Typical:</Highlight>** 3-4× Daily ATR OR below daily structure<br/>

**<Highlight variant="blue">Example:</Highlight>**

→ Daily ATR: 60 pips<br/>
→ Stop distance: 3 × 60 = 180 pips<br/>
→ Support at 1.0900, entry at 1.1080<br/>

**<Highlight variant="blue">STOP MANAGEMENT:</Highlight>**

**<Highlight variant="blue">Initial:</Highlight>** 3-4× Daily ATR below entry<br/>

**As trade progresses:**

→ Day 1-2: Keep original stop<br/>
→ Day 3+: Once +1R profit, trail stop to breakeven<br/>
→ Day 5+: Trail stop to 2× Daily ATR below current price<br/>

**<Highlight variant="blue">Weekend protocol:</Highlight>**

→ If position up >1R: Consider closing 50% before weekend<br/>
→ If position near entry: Reduce size 30-50% before weekend<br/>
→ If position negative: Close entirely before weekend (unless very high conviction)<br/>

**<Highlight variant="yellow">DAILY/WEEKLY/MONTHLY LIMITS:</Highlight>**

**<Highlight variant="blue">WEEKLY LIMITS (primary metric for swing traders):</Highlight>**
